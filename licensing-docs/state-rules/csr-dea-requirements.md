# CSR & DEA Requirements

Controlled Substance Registration (CSR) and DEA registration requirements by state for Fountain Vitality providers.

## Overview

Fountain Vitality providers prescribe Schedule III–V controlled substances (testosterone, some GLP-1 adjacent compounds). This requires:

1. **Federal DEA Registration** — one registration per state where CS are prescribed
2. **State Controlled Substance Registration (CSR)** — required in most states in addition to DEA
3. Some states require CSR before DEA can be obtained; others are concurrent

> ⚠️ Always verify current DEA and CSR requirements directly with the state board before applying. Rules change frequently.

---

## DEA Registration

### General Process
- Apply at [DEA Diversion Registration](https://www.deadiversion.usdoj.gov/webforms/dtStartRegistration.jsp)
- Fee: $888 per state (3-year registration as of 2025)
- Must have a valid state license before DEA can be issued
- Separate DEA number required for each state

### DEA Schedule Coverage
Fountain providers typically need **Schedules III–V** at minimum. Confirm with CMO whether Schedule II is required for any treatment protocols.

## State CSR Requirements

### Florida
- **CSR Required:** Yes
- **Board:** Florida Department of Health
- **Portal:** [floridahealth.gov](https://www.floridahealth.gov/licensing-and-regulation/index.html)
- **Fee:** ~$75
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years (with medical/nursing license)
- **Notes:** Florida CSR is tied to the state license renewal. Apply concurrently.

---

### Texas
- **CSR Required:** Yes
- **Board:** Texas Department of Public Safety (DPS) — Controlled Substances Act
- **Portal:** [txdps.state.tx.us](https://www.txdps.state.tx.us/rsd/csa/)
- **Fee:** ~$35
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years
- **Notes:** Texas DPS CSR is separate from the Board of Nursing/Medicine. Must be renewed independently.

---

### Georgia
- **CSR Required:** Yes
- **Board:** Georgia Drugs and Narcotics Agency (GDNA)
- **Portal:** [gdna.georgia.gov](https://gdna.georgia.gov)
- **Fee:** ~$25
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years
- **Notes:** GDNA permit required for all prescribers of Schedule II–V substances.

---

### Tennessee
- **CSR Required:** Yes
- **Board:** Tennessee Department of Health — Controlled Substances Monitoring Database
- **Fee:** ~$50
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years
- **Notes:** Tennessee also has a Controlled Substance Monitoring Program (CSMD) registration.

---

### Arizona
- **CSR Required:** No separate CSR — DEA registration covers CS prescribing
- **Notes:** Verify current AZ rules — some states have changed this recently.

---

### Colorado
- **CSR Required:** Yes
- **Board:** Colorado Department of Regulatory Agencies (DORA)
- **Fee:** ~$35
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years

## CSR Application Checklist

Before applying for a CSR in any state:

- [ ] State professional license is active and in good standing
- [ ] DEA registration is active (or being applied for concurrently)
- [ ] No disciplinary actions or pending investigations
- [ ] NPI number confirmed
- [ ] Practice address confirmed (matches DEA registration)
- [ ] Payment method ready
