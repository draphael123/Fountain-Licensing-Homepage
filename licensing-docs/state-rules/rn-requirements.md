# RN Licensing Requirements

State-by-state requirements for Registered Nurses (RN) practicing under Fountain Vitality's telehealth platform.

## General RN Licensing Notes

- **eNLC (Enhanced Nurse Licensure Compact)** allows RNs to hold one multi-state license valid in all compact member states
- If a nurse's home state is an eNLC member and they practice in another eNLC state, a separate license is NOT required
- If an RN moves to a new state, they must apply for a new home state license within 90 days
- Non-compact states require a separate full license regardless of home state

## eNLC Member States

As of 2025, 40+ states are eNLC members. Check [ncsbn.org/nurse-licensure-compact](https://www.ncsbn.org/nurse-licensure-compact.htm) for the current list.

## State Entries

### Florida
- **Board:** Florida Board of Nursing (FLBN)
- **eNLC Member:** Yes
- **Application Portal:** [floridasnursing.gov](https://floridasnursing.gov)
- **Fee:** ~$100 initial | ~$65 renewal
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years (odd years)
- **CE Required:** 24 hours
- **Notes:** If RN's home state is Florida and another state is eNLC, no additional license needed for that state.

---

### Texas
- **Board:** Texas Board of Nursing (TXBON)
- **eNLC Member:** Yes
- **Application Portal:** [bon.texas.gov](https://bon.texas.gov)
- **Fee:** ~$75 initial | ~$65 renewal
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years
- **CE Required:** 20 hours
- **Notes:** Texas is an eNLC compact member. Endorsement available for out-of-state nurses.

---

### Georgia
- **Board:** Georgia Board of Nursing
- **eNLC Member:** Yes
- **Application Portal:** [sos.ga.gov](https://sos.ga.gov)
- **Fee:** ~$75 initial | ~$45 renewal
- **Renewal Cycle:** Every 2 years (odd years)
- **CE Required:** 30 hours

## Non-Compact States — Full License Required

If Fountain operates in any non-compact states, full RN licensure by endorsement is required. Current non-compact states include California, New York, and others — verify at ncsbn.org.
